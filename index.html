Our Services

Accounting Bookkeeping

Our bookkeeping service helps record, classify, and summarise the financial transactions of your business on a regular basis. We help you to keep track of your financial performance and make informed decisions based on accurate financial data. We also provide services of managing accounts payable and receivable, reconciling bank statements, and producing financial statements.

Audit Engagement

Our audit engagement service provides an independent examination of your company's financial records and systems to assess the accuracy and compliance with the accounting standards and regulations. We help businesses to identify the areas of risks and opportunities for improving their financial reporting and operations.

Tax advisory

Our tax advisory service provides guidance and advices to clients on tax-related matters, such as tax planning, compliance with tax laws and regulations, and resolving tax issues. These help businesses to minimize their tax liability and manage their tax risks. We also work closely with the company's accountants and other advisors to ensure a comprehensive approach to tax management.

Company Secretary

Our company secretary service provides administrative supports to the board of directors and executive team to maintain corporate records, manage regulatory filings, ensure complianced with corporate governance requirements and operating in accordance with applicable laws and regulations and maintain good corporate governance practices. We also provide strategic advices and support to the company's leadership in risk management, corporate strategy, and stakeholder engagement.
